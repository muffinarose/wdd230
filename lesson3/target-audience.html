Target Audience

Description of Target Audience

The target audience consists of 2 main groups.
The first is small business owners. They are mostly 30-45 years old, middle-class, and married with children. They are looking to network with other business owners, and to advertise their businesses to residents and visitors.
The second group is residents of, and visitors to, the Pacific-Algona area. They have a wide range of ages from 20-55, and consist of both married and single people. Most are parents. They are looking for information about the local area and the businesses there.


Two Personas

Alice Oaks is 38 years old, Married with 3 children, Stephen-14, Geraldine-16, and Terrence-17. She owns a bakery in Pacific that specializes in cupcakes. Her husband, James, works nearby at Boeing as an electrician.
Caroline Styles is a 24 year-old stay-at-home-mom. She and her husband, Brad, who also works at Boeing, just moved to Algona 3 months ago from Kansas with their 2 daughters, Abigale-4, and Lucy-6.

Scenarios

1 - Alice wants to connect with other businesses and talk about collaborations. She goes to the chamber page to find out when and where the chamber's next networking meeting is.
2 - James tried to fix the upstairs toilet, and only made it worse. Now he needs to find a plumber to fix his mistakes. He wants to find a reputable one in the area. He goes to the chamber page to find business listings for local plumbers and handymen.
3 - It's a beautiful day and Caroline wants to take her daughters out in the rare sunshine. She goes onto the chamber page to find parks and other outdoor amenities in the area.
4 - Caroline wants to start a small home-business crocheting hats for dogs. She goes to the chamber page to find contacts to learn the local rules and laws for opening a home-business.
5 - Alice wants to sponsor a local youth sports team and provide treats after games, she goes to the chamber page to find out about sponsorship opportunities in the area.
