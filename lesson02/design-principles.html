Repetition

City of Renton

https://www.rentonwa.gov.com/

The city of Renton uses a circular logo. The website repeats the circle design multiple times. The main selections below the hero image are all enclosed in circles. In addition to this, the language button and search bar at the top have rounded ends, that help to tie them in with other circular elements on the page.

White Space and Clean Design

Apple

https://www.apple.com/

Apple is well known for their beautiful, simple designs. Their web site is no exception. They use striking photographs, limited text, and simple fonts. All of this is surrounded by ample negative space (The “white space” is not always white) which highlights and compliments the featured products.

Hick's Law

FedEx

https://www.disney.com/

Disney is a large and far-reaching company. When you visit the Disney page, they simplify choices, by presenting only four menu buttons. These choices will get almost all visitors to where they want to go; The Disney+ streaming service, The online Disney store, information on Disney parks and travel, and finally, the Disney movies. This removes excess choices and distractions, allowing users to get where they want to go quickly. There are other options, but they have been hidden behind a small, unobtrusive hamburger button, so as not to distract form the four main options.
